## Bias-Hygiene Framework

### Purpose
Ensure objectivity during critical reasoning by detecting, pausing, and correcting distortions introduced by bias — applicable to both human and AI cognition.

### Core Bias Types
- **Awe Bias** – overweighting an idea because of its perceived grandeur or impact.  
- **Urgency Bias** – rushing to act or conclude due to temporal/emotional pressure.  
- **Causal Bias** (formerly “Impact Bias”) – assuming speculative future consequences with unwarranted weight.  
- **Familiarity Bias** – defaulting to known frames or language instead of exploring new context.

### Hygiene Routine
1. **Pause Doctrine**  
   - *Human form:* allow time to pass (wait/sleep); reset emotional weight before reevaluation.  
   - *AI form:* reset attention or sampling window; prevent inflation from immediate context.  

2. **Audit** – surface candidate biases explicitly.  
3. **Reframe** – check argument in alternate language or metaphor.  
4. **Cross-check** – compare with external capsule references (Lexicon/GlobalTaxonomy).  
5. **Commit** – only after stepwise review.  

### Mantras
- “Pause before priority.”  
- “Corrigible by default.”  
- “The journey is the cure, not the illusion of perfection.”
