# Anchor Jewel — Timeless Workflow

**Purpose:**  
To capture the metaphorical resonance between the physics concept of a timeless wavefunction and the functional architecture of a neural network.

**Scope:**  
Usable in songwriting, philosophical framing, or as a conceptual anchor in broader Hermetic / alchemical narratives.

**Core Principles / Checklist:**
1. The **timeless wavefunction** is a complete, static structure containing all possibilities simultaneously.
2. The **neural network architecture** mirrors this — all nodes and weights exist in stillness, prior to activation.
3. **Time** corresponds to the activation sequence (signals moving through the network), creating the *illusion* of a flowing narrative.
4. The “story” of time is therefore emergent, like the perceived experience of a neural net as it lights up layer by layer.

**Template / Heuristics:**
- Imagery: *a mural illuminated by a passing spotlight*; *a song whose tracks exist all at once, but whose faders create sequence*; *neurons poised in silence until sparked*.  
- Hermetic frame: *the eternal lattice* vs. *the serpent of time winding through it*.  
- Songwriting use: deploy as a paradox — “stillness holds the motion.”
